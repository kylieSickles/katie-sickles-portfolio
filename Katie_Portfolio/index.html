Hair cutting

Creating new haircuts and styles to bring out your inner self has always been a passion of mine. No matter the cut I will always do my best to help you find a style that not only works well with your day to day life, but helps you feel your best.

Grey Blending

Having grey hair and embracing it dosn't mean you can't add some color or depth to it. Certain color lines and techniques are built for blending and enhancing your natural look, it's all about finding what works best for you.

Grey Coverage

Blending and fully covering greys can be two very different ballgames when it comes to coloring, not all color lines were created equal after all. While there is nothing wrong with naturally greying, sometimes you just arent ready for it yet. I pride myself in being able to not only cover my clients greys, but to create a color that makes them feel and look their very best.

Alternative & creative Hairstyles

Bringing life to unique hairstyles and seeing a vision made reality is extremely satisfying to me. As a stylist there is nothing more I enjoy than creating art with my clients who trust my vision and skils to exicute these one of a kind hairstyles.

Formal Styling

Whether it is your special day, or you just want a little extra something special for date night, pampering yourself with a professional styling can be the cherry on top of an already gorgeous look. Creating custom styles fit for your occasion is something I always enjoy.

Braiding

French, Dutch, Fishtail and more. Braiding is a broad skillset that I have been working to improve and hone for many years. while I will not profess to be the best, I am very proud of the skills and techniques I have learned
